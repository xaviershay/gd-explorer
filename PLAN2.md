End goal: see the impact different gear options has on my character, from a number of diffecent perspectives (damage, defence, resistances, etc)

Steps to get there:
* Calculate resistances from gear, taking into account the cap, penalty from difficulty (CLI option), and any +% max resistance modifiers.
* Calculate OA, DA and weapon damage from gear.
* Include buffs from devotions
* Include buffs from skills - Grim Tools (comparison tool) lets you toogle each of permanent, temporary, and proc'ed buffs.
* Ability to "overlay" a given item(s) onto a character. Use the base character but then using the given item(s) instead.
